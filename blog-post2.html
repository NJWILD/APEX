Master the Hoodie Layering Game
Back to Blog

Layering with hoodies is both stylish and functional — perfect for chilly evenings and a bold urban aesthetic. Here’s how to get it right.

Choose Neutral Base Colors

A black or grey hoodie is a versatile piece to layer over colorful tees or under vibrant jackets.

Experiment with Lengths

Let your longer shirt peek out from under your hoodie to create contrast. This adds dimension to your outfit.

Layer with Overshirts or Bombers

A checked overshirt or bomber jacket layered over a hoodie instantly elevates your style. Keep the hoodie hood out for the full effect.

Footwear Matters

Pair with high-top sneakers or boots depending on the vibe. Streetwear is all about coordination.
